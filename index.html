Welcome

Hi, I am a systems researcher working at VMware Research (VRG). I'm interested in Operating Systems design and implementation, networks and system software interaction with emerging hardware technologies.

I graduated with a Doctor of Sciences in Computer Science from ETH ZÃ¼rich in 2017. My thesis involved (among other things) hacking on the Barrelfish Project.

In 2010, I co-founded a start-up called Texercises, a collaborative exercise database for teachers and students. Texercises was acquired by edTechLab AG in 2015 (see taskbase.com).

Latest announcements

  • We released the nrkernel as an open-source project. The project explores the design of a new OS kernel that uses operation logging, flat-combining and replication to improve scalability of OS data-structures.
  • I'm holding the 2nd part of the Rust workshop on March 31st at DevPulseCon 2021 together with Ben Pfaff.
  • Ben Pfaff and I held a Rust workshop at DevPulseCon 2020.
  • We released node-replication: A library to transform single-threaded data-structures into concurrent ones.
